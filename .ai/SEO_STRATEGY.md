# SEO Optimization Plan — DocsVault

To dominate search results for "pitch deck", "docsend alternative", and "secure document sharing", we will implement a multi-layered SEO strategy focusing on high-intent comparison pages and technical metadata.

## 1. Targeted Keywords
- **Core Product**: `DocSend alternative`, `open source docsend`, `secure document sharing`, `pitch deck analytics`.
- **Verticals**: `share pitch deck safely`, `investor deck tracking`, `contract sharing with analytics`.
- **Competitor-Specific**: `DocSend vs Papermark vs DocsVault`, `is DocSend secure`, `Papermark pricing alternative`.

## 2. Comparison Strategy
We will create dedicated "vs" pages to capture users searching for alternatives.
- **[NEW] `app/vs/docsend/page.js`**: "DocsVault vs DocSend: Why Founders are Switching".
- **[NEW] `app/vs/papermark/page.js`**: "The Better Open-Source Alternative to Papermark".

## 3. Technical Implementation
- **Layout Metadata**: Update `app/layout.js` with global SEO tokens (Title, Description, OpenGraph).
- **JSON-LD Schema**: Add `SoftwareApplication` and `FAQPage` schema to the landing page.
- **Semantic HTML**: Ensure all headers (H1-H3) follow a strict keyword hierarchy.

## 4. Content Clusters
- **Blog/Resources**: (Future) "How to Create a Pitch Deck that Wins Rounds".
- **AI Integration**: Highlight "AI-Powered Q&A on Documents" as a unique differentiator that competitors lack.
